Privacy & Data

ActiveCanvas is designed to keep your content local first. This page outlines what we collect and how to stay fully in control.

Local-first Architecture

Default

By default, your pages, assets, and edits stay on your machine. Exports are static HTML/CSS with no telemetry or trackers embedded. Your content never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to deploy it.

AI Integrations

Optional

If you connect an AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini), prompts and page context are sent only to that provider. We do not store, log, or access your AI conversations.

Disable AI features to keep everything completely offline. The editor works fully without any AI connection.

Self-hosted Deployments

Enterprise

For teams requiring data residency, self-host the editor and point it to your chosen AI endpoint. We do not receive logs, metrics, or any data from self-hosted installations. Full control, full privacy.

No Hidden Tracking

We don't embed analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts in your exported pages. What you build is yours—clean, fast, and private. No surprise cookies, no behavioral tracking.

Questions?

We're committed to transparency. If you have questions about data handling or need clarification on our privacy practices, reach out anytime.
