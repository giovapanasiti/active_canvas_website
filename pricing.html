Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers before you commit.

Can I self-host?

Yes. Use the Community edition for local or private installs. Enterprise adds support, SSO, and compliance.

Do I lose work if AI is off?

No. All edits are regular HTML/CSS. AI is additive; nothing relies on a remote runtime.

How are templates licensed?

Templates ship under MIT alongside the project. Use commercially, modify, and export freely.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Your exported HTML is always yours to keep.
